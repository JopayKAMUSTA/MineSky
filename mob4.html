Zombie

Overview

Zombies are one of the most common hostile mobs in Minecraft. They spawn in dark or dimly lit areas and attack players and villagers on sight. With a humanoid form and groaning sounds, zombies are often the first enemy new players encounter.

Health & Damage

  • Health: 20 (10 hearts)
  • Attack Damage: 2 to 5 depending on game difficulty

Weaknesses

  • Burns in direct sunlight (unless wearing a helmet or standing in shade).
  • Slow movement speed makes it easier to outrun.
  • Vulnerable to all standard weapons (swords, bows, axes, etc.).
  • Can be repelled by Iron Golems and Snow Golems.

Additional Traits

  • Can break down wooden doors on hard difficulty.
  • May spawn with armor or tools, especially on harder difficulties.
  • Has a chance to summon reinforcements when attacked (on hard difficulty).
  • Variants include Baby Zombies, Zombie Villagers, and Husk (desert variant).
