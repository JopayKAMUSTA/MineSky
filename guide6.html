What is Redstone?

Redstone is Minecraft's version of electrical wiring. It allows players to build powerful circuits, machines, and automated systems.

How to Obtain

  • Mine Redstone Ore found deep underground (below level 16).
  • Each block drops 4-5 Redstone Dust when mined with an iron pickaxe or better.

Uses

- Create redstone circuits to power doors, traps, and machines.
- Craft items like compasses, clocks, powered rails, and observers.
- Build complex contraptions like farms, elevators, and even computers!

Fun Fact

Redstone behaves like electricity, but with unique Minecraft mechanics, such as signal strength and repeaters!
