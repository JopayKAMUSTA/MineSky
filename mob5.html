Enderman

Overview

The Enderman is a tall, mysterious creature from Minecraft. It has dark black skin, glowing purple eyes, and can teleport. It becomes hostile when looked at directly.

Health

40 HP (20 Hearts)

Weaknesses

  • Water and Rain (takes damage)
  • Snowballs (no damage, but gets agitated)
  • Direct Eye Contact (triggers hostility)
