Spider

What is a Spider?

The Spider is a hostile mob in Minecraft, famous for its red glowing eyes and ability to climb walls. Spiders spawn in dark areas and become dangerous at night.

Stats

  • Health: 16 (8 hearts)
  • Attack Damage: 2-4 (depends on difficulty)
  • Drops: String, Spider Eye (occasionally)

Behavior

- Hostile at night or in dark caves.
- Neutral during daytime unless provoked.
- Can climb walls and leap at players.
