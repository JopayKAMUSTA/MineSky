What is an Enchanting Table?

An Enchanting Table is a block in Minecraft used to apply enchantments to gear using Lapis Lazuli and experience levels.

How to Craft an Enchanting Table

  • 4 Obsidian
  • 2 Diamonds
  • 1 Book

How to Use

  1. Right-click the Enchanting Table.
  2. Place the item to enchant (e.g., a sword).
  3. Add 1-3 Lapis Lazuli.
  4. Select one of the three available enchantments (requires XP).

Boosting Power with Bookshelves

To unlock higher-tier enchantments, surround the table with up to 15 bookshelves:

  • Leave a 1-block gap around the table.
  • Bookshelves must be at the same height or one block higher.
  • Do not block the path with other blocks.

Common Enchantments

Item Enchantment Effect
Sword Sharpness Increases damage
Pickaxe Efficiency Faster mining
Armor Protection Reduces all damage
Bow Power Increases arrow damage
All Gear Unbreaking Slows durability loss

Tips

  • Use the grindstone to remove unwanted enchantments.
  • Combine enchanted books using an anvil for stronger results.
  • Enchant books first to avoid wasting gear durability.
  • Reset enchantments by enchanting a trash item (like a wooden shovel).
