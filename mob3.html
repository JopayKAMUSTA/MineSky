Iron Golem

Overview

The Iron Golem is a large, strong, neutral mob in Minecraft, designed to protect villagers and players from hostile threats. Built from iron blocks and a carved pumpkin, this powerful entity roams villages, defending against zombies, skeletons, and more.

Behavior

Iron Golems patrol villages and attack hostile mobs by delivering massive melee hits. They are passive toward players unless provoked. When damaged, Iron Golems show cracks on their body but can be repaired using iron ingots.

Spawning & Utility

Iron Golems naturally spawn in villages when enough villagers are present and meet specific conditions. Players can also create them manually using four iron blocks and a carved pumpkin. They’re commonly used in iron farms or to safeguard villagers.

Fun Facts

  • Iron Golems can hold poppies and offer them to baby villagers.
  • They deal up to 21 damage per hit — one of the strongest melee mobs in Minecraft.
  • Unlike most mobs, they don't take fall damage.
